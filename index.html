About us

The Plateau State Ministry of Commerce is dedicated to promoting economic growth and development in the state. As part of our efforts to create a conducive business environment, we have launched this online portal for the registration of business premise.
  • Convenience: Register your business premise from the comfort of your own office or home.
  • Time-saving: Reduce the time spent on manual registration.
  • Cost-effective: Save money on transportation and other costs associated with manual registration.
  • Transparency: Get instant updates on the status of your registration.

    • Our mission is to provide a seamless and efficient registration process for businesses operating in Plateau State. We aim to simplify the process of registering business premise, reducing the time and cost associated with manual registration.

    Our vision is to create a thriving business community in Plateau State, where entrepreneurs and businesses can operate with ease and confidence. We envision a future where businesses can focus on growth and innovation, without being hindered by bureaucratic processes.

    Why Registration with Plateau State Business Premise Portal

    Reasons why the Plateau State Ministry of commerce Business Premise Registration Portal is Important

    The online portal simplifies the business premise registration process, reducing the time and effort required to register a business. it also provides a transparent and accountable process, minimizing the risk of corruption and ensuring that all businesses are fairly.

    By encouraging business registration and compliance, the portal helps formalize more enterprises, increasing the government’s ability to support local businesses. This fosters a regulated environment that attracts investment, stimulates entrepreneurship, and contributes to sustainable economic development in Plateau State.

    The system enables the government to collect accurate, real-time data on registered businesses. This improves policy formulation, resource allocation, and strategic planning, allowing for more targeted support to business sectors and improved service delivery.

    Announcement

    To our esteemed Tax Payers on the Plateau, you are required by law to register and renew your business premises permit under the registration of business law 1979 and 1983 as amended in the Plateau State revenue consolidation law 2020. All business owners are expected to proceed with the registration and download the Demand Notice before making payment. You can pay to our accredited agent POS terminal, pay through the Plateau IGR portal online or visit the PSIRS or Ministry of Commerce & Industry.
