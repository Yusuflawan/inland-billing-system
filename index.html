About us

The Plateau State Ministry of Commerce is dedicated to promoting economic growth and development in the state. As part of our efforts to create a conducive business environment, we have launched this online portal for the registration of business premises.
  • Convenience: Register your business premises from the comfort of your own office or home.
  • Time-saving: Reduce the time spent on manual registration.
  • Cost-effective: Save money on transportation and other costs associated with manual registration.
  • Transparency: Get instant updates on the status of your registration.

    • Our mission is to provide a seamless and efficient registration process for businesses operating in Plateau State. We aim to simplify the process of registering business premises, reducing the time and cost associated with manual registration.

    Our vision is to create a thriving business community in Plateau State, where entrepreneurs and businesses can operate with ease and confidence. We envision a future where businesses can focus on growth and innovation, without being hindered by bureaucratic processes.
